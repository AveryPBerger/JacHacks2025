Game Info

Welcome to Water Waster - a game the teaches you about the impact that everday desicions have on your use of water. This game is a point-and-click adventure, where you are on your day off, giving you time to do all sorts of things around your house. But be careful, the game will calculate how much water you've used by the end of the day, so make sure you remember to be environmentally conscous of the actions you take on your day off.
